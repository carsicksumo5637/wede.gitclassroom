Play It Forward

Empowering Young Athletes, Inspiring Future Champions.


About Play It Forward Foundation Founded by passionate rugby player Nathi Ntuli, the Play It Forward Foundation is committed to transforming young lives through sports. Inspired by his own journey and fueled by a desire to see others succeed, Nathi established the foundation to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity.

Our Story: The Play It Forward Foundation began with a simple yet powerful vision: to support and develop young athletes from all backgrounds, including those with disabilities. Recognizing the transformative power of sports, our foundation provides scholarships and training programs to ensure that every child has the chance to excel and achieve their dreams.
