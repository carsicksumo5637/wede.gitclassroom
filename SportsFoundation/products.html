Play It Forward

Empowering Young Athletes, Inspiring Future Champions.


Youth Training Programs:
o Rugby Development Camps: Intensive training camps focusing on developing fundamental skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship in young rugby players. Open to both abled and disabled athletes.
o Multi-Sport Clinics: Comprehensive training sessions that introduce children to various sports, helping them discover and develop their athletic talents.

• School Partnerships:
o Sports Education Integration: Collaborate with schools to integrate sports programs into the curriculum, promoting physical education and active lifestyles.
o After-School Programs: Provide structured after-school sports programs that offer students a safe and productive environment to hone their athletic skills.

2. Scholarships and Bursaries:
• Athletic Scholarships: o Sports Excellence Bursaries: Financial support for talented young athletes from disadvantaged backgrounds to attend specialized sports schools or programs.
o Inclusive Sports Scholarships: Scholarships specifically for athletes with disabilities, ensuring that all children have the opportunity to pursue their sports dreams.
